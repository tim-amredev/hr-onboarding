Help & Support

Welcome to the HR Onboarding Portal help section. Here you'll find answers to common questions and guidance on completing your onboarding process.

Frequently Asked Questions

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot password?" link on the login page. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

Don't worry! Your progress is automatically saved as you complete each section. You can log out and return later to continue where you left off.

After submitting your equipment request, please allow 5-7 business days for processing and delivery. You'll receive a confirmation email when your equipment is shipped.

Your onboarding progress is displayed on your dashboard. When all sections show as "Completed," your onboarding process is finished. You'll also receive a confirmation email from HR.

You can update your personal information at any time by going to the Personal Information section. Submit the form with your updated information, and HR will be notified of the changes.

Onboarding Process Guide

  1. Complete Personal Information
    Provide your contact details and emergency contact information.
    Step 1
  2. Review and Acknowledge Policies
    Read and acknowledge all company policies and procedures.
    Step 2
  3. Complete Training Modules
    Complete all required training modules for your role.
    Step 3
  4. Request Equipment
    Submit requests for any equipment you need for your role.
    Step 4
  5. Enroll in Benefits
    Select your benefits options and complete enrollment forms.
    Step 5

Important Note

You must complete all steps within 30 days of your start date. If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact HR.
