Company History

American Remodeling Enterprises was founded in 2005 by John and Sarah Thompson with a vision to provide high-quality remodeling services with exceptional customer service. What began as a small family business has grown into one of the region's most respected remodeling companies.

Key Milestones
  • 2005: Company founded
  • 2010: Expanded to commercial projects
  • 2015: Added Epoxy Floor division
  • 2018: Opened second location
  • 2022: Reached 100 employees
Our Specialties
  • Residential Remodeling
  • Commercial Renovations
  • Epoxy Floor Installation
  • Custom Cabinetry
  • Outdoor Living Spaces

Company Today

Today, American Remodeling Enterprises serves customers throughout the region with a team of skilled professionals dedicated to quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction. Our Epoxy Floor Experts division has become an industry leader in commercial and residential epoxy flooring solutions.

Did You Know?

American Remodeling Enterprises has completed over 5,000 projects since our founding, with a customer satisfaction rate of 98%.

Key Company Policies

Below is a summary of important policies. For complete details, please refer to the Employee Handbook.

Safety Policy

Safety is our top priority. All employees must:

  • Complete required safety training
  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE)
  • Report unsafe conditions immediately
  • Follow all safety protocols and procedures
  • Participate in regular safety meetings
Remember: You have the right and responsibility to stop work if you believe conditions are unsafe.
Customer Service Standards

Our reputation depends on exceptional customer service. All employees should:

  • Respond to customer inquiries within 24 hours
  • Maintain a professional appearance and demeanor
  • Communicate clearly and respectfully
  • Follow up after project completion
  • Escalate issues to management when necessary
Quality Standards

We maintain rigorous quality standards for all work:

  • Follow established procedures and specifications
  • Conduct quality checks at key project milestones
  • Document all work performed
  • Address quality issues immediately
  • Participate in continuous improvement initiatives
Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to:

  • Act with integrity and honesty
  • Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect
  • Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information
  • Avoid conflicts of interest
  • Report violations of company policies
Policy Resources

For complete policy information, please refer to:

  • Employee Handbook (available in HR Portal)
  • Safety Manual (available in each location)
  • Department-specific procedure guides
